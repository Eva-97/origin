Once upon a time in the Kingdom of Camelot
. 
There lived a young king named Arthur on whose shoulder lies the destiny and redemption of Camelot. 
He had an evil uncle called Sax who torments the Kingdom of Camelot with sorcery and his army of the dead.
The young king sought to put an end to his uncle'sevil deeds and also avenge his father who was killed by one of Sax's warlord, Mars.
